Privacy Policy

Artifacts does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

Local Data

The app stores artifact checklist state, found timestamps, and optional notes locally on your device so it can show collection progress. This information is not sent to the developer or to third parties.

Accounts, Analytics, Ads, And Tracking

Artifacts does not use accounts, analytics, advertising, tracking, third-party SDKs, or cloud sync.

Device Permissions

The app does not request access to location, contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or other protected device resources.

Future Changes

If a future version adds online features or third-party services, this privacy policy and the App Store privacy disclosures will be updated before those features are released.

Unofficial Status

Artifacts is an unofficial fan-made companion reference app. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or approved by GSC Game World.
